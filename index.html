Welcome to MIR Platform v2.0
Step 1 of 4 — What is MIR?
MIR is a decentralised sovereign intelligence platform. Analyse geopolitical scenarios, earn reputation (FRS), mine MIR tokens, and trade peer-to-peer — all without any central server controlling your account.
Your identity is a cryptographic keypair generated locally in your browser. No email. No password. No recovery service.
Step 2 of 4 — Your Public Key
🔓 Public Key = Your Address
Your public key is like a bank account number — safe to share with anyone. Others use it to send you MIR and verify your identity.
✓ Share it freely  ·  ✓ Receive MIR with it  ·  ✓ Post it publicly
Step 3 of 4 — Your Private Key
🔑 Private Key = Your Permanent Password
Your private key is the only proof you own your account. Generated once, locally, and never sent anywhere.
MIR is fully decentralised. If you lose your private key, your account and all funds are permanently and irrecoverably lost. There is no reset, no support, no exception. Write it down or use EXPORT BACKUP immediately after registration.
Step 4 of 4 — Lost Your Key?
You can always re-register with a new keypair. Your new identity is a fresh sovereign partition — a completely independent account. Old data tied to the lost key is inaccessible by design.
Best practice: After registering, click EXPORT in your wallet and save an encrypted .json backup to an offline device.
